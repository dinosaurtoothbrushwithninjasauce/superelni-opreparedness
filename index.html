MULTI-HAZARD THREAT ASSESSMENT

HIGH RISK

SEVERE DROUGHT

Central America, Caribbean, Philippines, Australia, Indonesia. Agricultural losses and water scarcity.

  • Crop failure & food insecurity
  • Water rationing & reservoir depletion
  • Wildfire risk amplification
HIGH RISK

CATASTROPHIC FLOODING

Southern US, Peru, Ecuador, Southern Brazil, Uruguay. Infrastructure damage and landslides.

  • River overflow & flash floods
  • Mudslides & structural collapse
  • Waterborne disease outbreaks
EXTREME

EXTREME HEAT

Global temperature records. Heat stress on populations, livestock, and power grids.

  • Heatstroke & mortality spikes
  • Grid failure from AC overload
  • Livestock & crop heat stress
SYSTEMIC

SUPPLY CHAIN COLLAPSE

Compounded by geopolitical fuel/fertilizer shortages. Rice and staple grain disruptions.

  • Fertilizer scarcity (Iran conflict)
  • Fuel price spikes & transport halt
  • Export controls & rationing
MODERATE

PACIFIC TYPHOONS

Atlantic cyclones may decrease, but Pacific storm formation intensifies with warmer seas.

  • Intensified Pacific hurricanes
  • Storm surge & coastal flooding
  • Single landfall = devastation
ELEVATED

HEALTH CRISES

Water scarcity, heat stress, and displacement create vector-borne disease vectors.

  • Mosquito-borne disease spread
  • Respiratory issues from haze/smoke
  • Contaminated water diseases
